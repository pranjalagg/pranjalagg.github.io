  • 2021.01 - 2021.05

    • Trained a Natural Language Processing (NLP) based system to classify text comments as Hope, Non-Hope speech

    • Intriduced a third label (Neutral Speech) for better classification
    • Analyzes and experimented on both the datsasets (old and new) with conventional ML models, LSTMs, and adopted BERT Architecture
    • Published as a book chapter in Cyber Crime in Social Media (CCSM), August 2022.
